BetterReads

Description: Are you an author looking to grow your readership? Or are you a reader, searching for your next favorite book? Welcome to BetterReads, a site where you can do just that!

BetterReads was created to help connect authors and readers together. Authors can upload information on their books to the site and can post events like book signings or Q&As. Readers can then browse those events and attend ones they are interested in. They can also browse books in search of their next literary quest!

Role: I was in charge of the frontend aspects of the project. I used Tailwind for the css, Figma for the objects like the bookshelf, banners, and the bookmark on the account pages, and Google Icons for the icons on the landing and contact pages. I also assisted my partner on the backend, creating CRUD routes and helping with debugging.

Park-a-pedia

Description: Ever wanted to know more about the many natural/historical sites across the US? Now you can! Park-a-pedia is a place where users can search for a specific park, if they know the name, grab a random park, or search by state. Each page for an individual park contains information like the phone number, address, any alerts for that location, and a description. Find your next adventure on Park-a-pedia!
